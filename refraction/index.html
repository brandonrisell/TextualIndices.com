Refraction

Refraction is a puzzle game focusing on the players ability to solve puzzle’s with colored lasers.

  • Your Goal: Get a a properly colored laser to each of the required checkpoints.
  • Your Tools: Prisms and Mirrors
  • Your Obstacles: Walls, Doors, Switches and Wormholes

The concept is simple, but the levels are complex.
